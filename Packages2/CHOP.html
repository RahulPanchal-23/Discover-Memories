Chopta Tungnath

Nestled amongst the aesthetic natural bounties, Bir is a small village in Himachal Pradesh which is also known as the paragliding capital of India. Bir is famous for enthralling activities including trekking, paragliding, mountain biking, and hand gliding, giving you the adrenaline rush you’ve been looking for. For the ones looking for a thrill-filled vacation to challenge your inner adventurer, Bir won’t disappoint you at all. Bir has something for everyone; head to the Bir Road market to quench your shopping cravings, leisure walk along the lake, enjoy few hours at the Himalayan Film school, explore the cafes that serve divine local Tibetan dishes, and watch the tranquilising sight as the sun sets in the sky gleaming with different hues.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0th Day: Drive from Delhi to Chopta via Rishikesh (450 KM drive)
1st Day: Reach Chopta and Check in to Camps
2nd Day: Trek from Chopta to Tungnath Chandrashila
3rd Day: Chopta to Sari to Deoriatal and Back to Delhi

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Reach Chopta


Leave Delhi at 7PM, Pick up from Kashmiri Gate/Majnu ka Tila (Day 0). Enroute Chopta don't forget to take pictures at the confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers at Devprayag. Reach Chopta around noon & Check in to camps, freshen-up. Go for a village walk and visit the riverside to Aakash-Kamini. In the evening, enjoy warm bonfire and dinner. Overnight stay in camps.


DAY 2 : Tungnath Chandrashila trek


Rise before the sun to experience golden sunrise. After Breakfast, Begin your trek to Tungnath. The Tungnath Temple is one of the Panch Kedar temples and is the highest temple of Lord Shiva in the world. Trek further to magnificent Chandrashila Peak, It offers perfect panoramic views of remarkable peaks such as Kedarnath, Mandani, Chaukhamba etc. Come back to the campsite by evening, Dinner and overnight sleep.


DAY 3 :  Deoriatal trek | Departure


Wake up and get mesmerized by the breathtaking sunrise. Post breakfast, head to Sari Village. Begin your trek to mesmerizing Deoriatal Lake. Reach Deroital and enjoy the astonishing view. Trek down to Sari and start your return journey to Delhi. Reach Delhi the next morning with Beautiful memories.
