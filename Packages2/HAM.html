Hampta Pass

Hampta Pass is a crossover trek where the scenery keeps changing as it starts from the lush greenery of Kullu region and ends in the arid desert of Spiti. This is an all in one trek where you go through alpine forests of (Maple, Brich, Oak, Deodar) and enter the wide meadows, trekking next to the streams and once you reach the start of the snowline it’s a steep ascend all the way to the pass. You are treated to a unique variety of exotic mountain wildflowers all through the trek. Hampta Pass is in a small corridor between Lahaul's Chandra Valley and the Kullu valley of Himachal Pradesh, India. This pass is frequently used by shepherds of the lower Himalayan region, seeking high altitude grasslands in the summer, when the dry cold desert of Lahaul is barren.

Short Tentative Itinerary

1st Day: Manali Arrival | Drive to Jobri | Trek to Chika (9800 Feet)
2nd Day: Chika to Balu Ka Gera Trek (12,411 feet)
3rd Day: Balu Ka Gera to Sheagoru Trek (12,254 Feet) via Hampta Pass (14,035 Feet)
4th Day: Sheagoru to Chattru Trek (10,898 feet) | Chattru to Chandratal Drive and Back
5th Day: Drive from Chattru to Manali | Tour Ends

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Manali Arrival | Trek to Chika (9800 Feet)


Meet at the pick-up point by 08:00 AM. Head out on a drive from Manali to Jobri. On reaching Jobri, start trek and will head out for Chika. Post-check-in, lunch will be served. The rest of the day is for acclimatization and resting after the first set of the trek is completed by the group. Dinner will be served in the evening followed by an overnight stay in Chika.


DAY 2 : Chika to Balu Ka Gera Trek (12,411 feet)


Post Breakfast, head out on a trek to Balu Ka Ghera from Chika. All along the journey, you'll be accompanied by a river stream that follows the same path as the trek and the Indrasan peak is also visible through the trek. Post-check-in at the Balu Ka Ghera, lunch will be served. Dinner will be served in the evening followed by an overnight stay in Balu Ka Ghera.


DAY 3 : Balu Ka Gera to Sheagoru Trek (12,254 Feet) via Hampta Pass (14,035 Feet)


Get up early and have breakfast as this is one of the most important days of the trek. Post breakfast, head out to the Sheagoru campsite. Midway, cover Hampta Pass, which is the main attraction of the whole trek. Post-check-in at Sheagoru, have lunch. The rest of the day is at leisure and you're suggested to recharge your batteries for the next day. Dinner will be served in the evening and overnight stay in Shegoru.


DAY 4 : Sheagoru to Chattru Trek (10,898 feet) | Chattru to Chandratal Drive and Back


Post breakfast, start trekking towards Chattru. Upon reaching Chattru, take a drive to Chandratal. After a sightseeing tour, head back to Chattru. Post-check-in at Chattru, lunch will be served. Dinner will be served in the evening followed by an overnight stay in Chattru.


DAY 5 : Drive from Chattru to Manali | Tour Ends


Breakfast will be served in the morning. After that, we'll head out from Chattru towards Manali via Rohtang Pass/Atal Tunnel. The group will be disbanded upon reaching Manali. The Hampta Pass Trek ends here.
