Kalpa Chitkul Road Trip

There are several beautiful valleys in Himachal Pradesh such as Kullu Valley, Kangra Valley, Chamba Valley, Kinnaur Valley, etc. Out of all, Kinnaur is one of those valleys which remained untouched and is home to many picturesque places. Few of them are Kalpa, Sangla, Chitkul, etc. Kinnaur Valley is all about deep gorges, snowcapped mountain peaks, a blend of Indo-Tibetan culture, apple orchards, etc. Another amazing fact about Kinnaur Valley is that the region has the cleanest air in India. Kalpa is also known as Winter Abode of Lord Shiva and is famous for heavenly views of Kinnaur Kailash ranges and Roghi suicide point. Chitkul is the last village on the Indo Tibet border and is popular for breathtaking views of Baspa Valley. The road to Kinnaur Valley is exciting as it passes through natural mountain caves and its a part of Hindustan Tibet highway, which makes the road trip quite interesting.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0 Day: Departure from Delhi
1st Day: Reach Shimla and Drive to Narkanda
2nd Day: Narkanda to Chitkul
3rd Day: Chitkul to Kalpa
4th Day: Kalpa to Shimla and depart for Delhi.

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Transfer to Narkanda


Leave Delhi at 7PM (Day 0). In the morning reach Shimla have breakfast and freshen up . Drive to the Narkanda through a picturesque route. On arrival check in at hotel. Later leave for local sightseeing such as Hatu Peak. Enjoy mouthwatering dinner and Overnight stay at Narkanda.


DAY 2 : Chitkul (Last village of India)


Rise before the sun to experience golden sunrise. After Breakfast head towards Chitkul (The Last Village of India). Enroute Chitkul enjoy sightseeing at Sangla and Rakcham Village. After visiting, Check in at hotel and freshen up. Visit the most popular “Hindustan ka Akhri Dhaba.” Enjoy the dinner with a bonfire under the sky full of stars. Overnight stay at Chitkul.


DAY 3 : Journey to Kalpa


Wake up early morning, have breakfast and head towards Kalpa. The journey will be picturesque, and you can quench the thirst of photography. Don’t forget to get pictures at Kinnaur Suicide Point. Reach Kalpa by evening. Evening free for leisure. Enjoy dinner and Overnight stay at Kalpa.


DAY 4 : Kalpa to Shimla and depart for Delhi


Wake up early morning, have breakfast and head towards SHIMLA. On the way, have a look at Kinner Kailash peaks. Reach Shimla in the evening and depart for the overnight journey to Delhi. Reach Delhi the next morning with Beautiful memories.
