KASOL GRAHAN

Kasol is situated among the lush green pines of the Parvati Valley in the state of Himachal Pradesh. This place happens to be ideal for those who wish to laze around and pass the weekend sipping on some steaming cups of tea while watching the beauty of nature unfold before one's eyes. A trip to Kasol is like a breath of fresh air for residents of nearby cities that are usually bustling with activity. The village attracts tourists from all around the country and is becoming popular as a trekking destination with trails such as the Kheerganga trek, Tosh Valley Trek, and Malana Village trek among several others. Whether you want to camp by the riverside or visit Manikaran Sahib, Kasol can be a surprise package for anyone visiting the place for the first time. Engage with the villagers who are just as welcoming as the natural surroundings and try some local cuisine for a new experience. Kasol is definitely a treat for anyone on a budget with a great passion for travelling.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0th Day: Departure from Delhi
1st Day: Reach Kasol and Sightseeing.
2nd Day: Kasol to Grahan
3rd Day: Grahan and depart for Delhi.

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1 : KASOL SIGHTSEEING


Leave Delhi at 7PM, Pick up from Kashmiri Gate/Majnu ka Tila (Day 0). Reach Kasol by morning, Check-in to hotel and freshen up. Get ready to explore streets of Kasol, visit Chalal Bridge and chill along Parvati riverside. Try different cuisines at the local Hippie Cafe's. Back at the hotel by evening for Bonfire and Dinner. Overnight stay at Kasol.


DAY 2 : KASOL TO GRAHAN


Wake up early in the morning and enjoy the hot, fresh breakfast. Post breakfast, the much-awaited trek to Grahan Village begins! The total distance of the trek is 8 km and the trail passes through dense forest with a scenic view of Grahan nala (a tributary of Parvati river) along the trail. The village is pristine and remains covered with snow during winters. The village has many prestigious and overwhelming things to offer. After reaching there, Have dinner. Overnight stay in homestay/Camps.


DAY 3 : GRAHAN | DEPARTURE


Wake-up early in the morning and enjoy the beautiful sunrise. If time and weather allows, hike up to some of the nearby waterfalls. After having breakfast, we will visit two prestigious temples, and explore their local culture and traditions. Later start downhill trek towards Kasol. It takes around 3-4 hours to reach Kasol. Depart back to Delhi. Reach Delhi the next morning with Beautiful memories.
