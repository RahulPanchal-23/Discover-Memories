Dharamshala -Mcleodganj

Mcleodganj is a hill station near Dharamshala, popular among trekkers. Located in Kangra district, Mcleodganj's culture is a beautiful blend of Tibetan with some British influence.Also known as Little Lhasa and famous around the world for being home to the Tibetan spiritual leader Dalai Lama, Mcleod Ganj is a beautiful town situated near upper Dharamsala. Nestled amidst majestic hills and lush greenery, this town is culturally blessed by a prominent Tibetan influence owing to the major settlement of Tibetans here. Mcleodganj has one of the most mesmerising landscapes in the entire state of Himachal Pradesh and attracts a lot of tourists throughout the year. The towns of Dharamsala, Mcleodganj, Bhagsu Nag and Kangra are situated very close to each other and tourists must cover all these destinations while travelling here.

Short Tentative Itinerary

0 Day: Delhi - Mcleodganj
1st Day: Mcleodganj Arrival | Local Sightseeing
2nd Day: Dharamshala Sightseeing
3rd Day: Remaining Sightseeing | Departure

Tentative Itinerary


DAY 1: Mcleodganj Arrival | Local Sightseeing


Leave Delhi at 7PM, Pick up from pre pre-determined destination. Reach Mcleodganj by morning, Check-in to the hotel and freshen up. Post refreshment, visit Bhagsunag Waterfall and Bhagsunag Temple. Visit the famous Shiva Cafe and explore the local streets of Mcleodganj. Dinner and Overnight stay at Mcleodganj.


DAY 2 : Dharamshala Sightseeing


Wake up early in the morning and after breakfast get ready for a full day excursion to Dharamkot and Dharamshala. Visit Dalai Lama Monastery, Cricket Stadium, war memorial, museum and cafes of Dharamshala. Back to the hotel by evening. Dinner and Overnight stay at Mcleodganj.


DAY 3 : Departure


Get up early to enjoy the sunrise view. Have breakfast and check out from the hotel. Explore the market and cafes of Dharamshala. After lunch, depart back to Delhi. Reach Delhi the next morning with Beautiful memories.
